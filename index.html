Everest Base Camp: 14-Day Blueprint

DAY 01-02

The Gateway

Flight to Lukla and trek to Phakding, followed by the ascent to the vibrant Sherpa capital, Namche Bazaar.

DAY 03-05

Acclimatization & Culture

Rest days in Namche for altitude adjustment. Visit the Everest View Hotel and trek to Tengboche Monastery for spiritual blessings.

DAY 06-08

The High Valley

Ascend through Dingboche and Lobuche. The landscape shifts from lush forests to rugged alpine tundra and glacial moraines.

DAY 09-11

The Summit of the Trek

Venture to Gorak Shep, then reach Everest Base Camp (5,364m). Climb Kala Patthar for the most iconic view of Everest's peak.

DAY 12-14

The Descent

Trek back through Pheriche and Namche, descending to Lukla for your flight back to Kathmandu.

Questions? We Have Answers

What level of fitness is required for EBC?

While you don't need to be a marathon runner, good cardiovascular health and the ability to hike 6-7 hours a day with a small pack is essential.

What kind of gear do I need for Kyrgyzstan?

Layers are key. Temperatures can fluctuate wildly. We provide a full packing list upon booking, covering everything from thermal wear to off-road essentials.

Do you handle visa arrangements?

We provide full assistance and documentation for Nepal, Indonesia, and Kyrgyzstan visas to ensure a smooth entry process.
